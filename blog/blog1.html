Jesus often used parables—short, symbolic stories—to teach profound spiritual truths...

Why Did Jesus Use Parables?

“The knowledge of the secrets of the kingdom of heaven has been given to you, but not to them.” (Matthew 13:11, NIV)

Jesus used parables to reveal truth to those who were spiritually open while concealing it from those who refused to believe.

Living with a Biblical Mindset

  • Trusting in God’s Word, even when it challenges us.
  • Showing love, grace, and forgiveness.
  • Caring for others, as the Good Samaritan did.
  • Growing in faith, even if it starts small like a mustard seed.
